Pokemon Infinite - About

Pokemon Infinite is a brand new fan-created open-world Pokemon game in Unity, coded in C# for Mac and PC, using a modern approach to AAA game design and content. Our goal is to bring fans together once more, rectify mistakes made in the past in this terrific series, and push a new vision forward for the franchise! Explore a brand new world, fantastic new Pokemon, a more balanced competitve game, and a true spectacle of art, music, and story telling! Pokemon Infinite: Where Yesterday and Tomorrow Meet!
