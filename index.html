Pokemon Infinite - About

Pokemon Infinite is a brand new fan-created open-world Pokemon game in Unity, coded in C# for Mac and PC, using a modern approach to AAA game design and content. Our goal is to bring fans together once more, rectify mistakes made in the past in this terrific series, and push a new vision forward for the franchise! Explore a brand new world, fantastic new Pokemon, a more balanced competitve game, and a true spectacle of art, music, and story telling! Pokemon Infinite: Where Yesterday and Tomorrow Meet!

Brand New Mega Evolutions!

Just like the Kalos and Alola regions, Aumagari also holds the mysterious power of Mega Evolution! However, even more Pokemon here are able to bond with their trainers and transform beyond their limiations!

Huge new Gigantamax Forms!

Like the Galar Region, Aumagari experiences some of the power of Dynamax! While it's only 1.5x the power and the Pokemon don't grow to nearly the massive size they do in Galar, there are still Gigantamax Pokemon that call this region home, including brand new ones!

Exciting New Alternate Forms!

Aumagari is home to a fantastic spread of alternate forms of well known Pokemon, ranging from new Rotom appliances, to a special Unown form, try out the new versions of some of these powerful Pokemon!

Brand New Legendary Pokemon!

The main legendary of our game has an exclusive new Infinite Type! Mebiusan, the Temporal Causality Pokemon, has two forms, a Past and Future form. Using its ability Time Travel, it can transform into these forms at will!

Brand New Mechanical Changes!

One of the goals of Pokemon Infinite is to create a more balanced game where more Pokemon are able to be included in high competition. As such, there's many MANY changes that have been tested and approved for our game:

  • New Infinite Typing (Mebiusan, Apocalylidae, Arceus, Silvally, Castform)
  • HP Boosting Natures!
  • Three new permanent statuses! Blindness, Whiplash, and Bewitchment!
  • Changes to Abilities, Items, Base Stats, Moves, and Pokemon Learnsets!
  • Brand new Moves and Abilities!
  • Previously unreleased Pokemon are LEGAL!
  • Mechanical nerfs to Z-moves and Dynamax/Gigantamax!
  • Changes to the Burn and Poison status!
  • Nerfs of Weather and Terrains!
  • And much, much MORE!
